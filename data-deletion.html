User Data Deletion

R2U Gateway — Authentication Service

What we collect

R2U Gateway uses social login (e.g. Facebook, Google) exclusively for authentication purposes. The only information retrieved from social providers is your email address, used solely to identify you during the login session.

We do not store, share, or process any personal data beyond the active login session. No data is persisted in our systems after the session ends.

How to request deletion

Since no personal data is stored on our servers, there is nothing to delete. Your session data is automatically cleared when you log out or your session expires.

If you have any concerns or questions regarding your data, please contact us at:

privacy@tuodominio.com

Third party data

To revoke R2U Gateway's access to your social account, you can do so directly from your provider's settings:

— Facebook: Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
— Google: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps
